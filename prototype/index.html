Ingredients

Scorpion peppers, ghost peppers, cane sugar, fresh garlic, sea salt, apple cider vinegar, lime juice. Small-batch, hand-bottled in Naperville, IL.

Frequently asked questions

When will Scorch ship?

We're targeting late summer 2026 for our first batch. Waitlist members get first access and 20% off.

How hot is it really?

Roughly 250,000 SHU at the front, mellowing to a warm, savory finish. Hotter than sriracha, gentler than pure ghost pepper.

Is this vegan / gluten-free?

Yes to both. No animal products, no gluten, no artificial colors or preservatives.

Do you ship outside the US?

Not at launch — but international shipping is on the roadmap. Let us know on the waitlist where you'd like us to ship.

What if I can't handle the heat?

Full refund, no questions asked. We'd rather you find your right spice level than choke through ours.
